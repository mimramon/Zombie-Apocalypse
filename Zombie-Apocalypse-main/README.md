# Rules:
  - At the start of the game, you have 4 survivors
  - Aim is to survive 100 rounds
  - Each turn you roll a d6 to determine what event happens
  - Each value for a d6 represents an event:
    - Zombie - You encounter a random zombie
    - Resources - You find resources for crafting weapons and defences
    - Zombie - You encounter a random zombie
    - Food - You receive some amount of food
    - Nothing - Pretty self explanatory 
    - Survivor - You pick up a survivor to join your group, if you do not have enough space in your houses you cannot pick up the survivor, use the builder to make more houses

# Survivors:
  - Each survivor can have a certain class:
    - Hunter
    - Medic
    - Builder
    - Blacksmith

  - Different classes have different stats, the player will be able to upgrade the stats with a maximum stat level of 10. 
    - Food Consumption - Builder(5), Blacksmith(4), Hunter(3), medic(1)
    - Combat Strength - Hunter(5), Blacksmith(3), Builder(2), Medic(1)
    - Scavenging - Hunter(4), Medic(3), Blacksmith(1), Builder(1)

# Zombies:
  - Each Zombie can have a certain class:
    - Charger:  Low Damage 
    - Volatile Charger: Medium damage
    - Toxic Charger: High Damage
